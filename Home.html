Special Window for Re-lodgement of Transfer Requests of Physical Shares

SEBI circular No. : SEBI/HO/MIRSD/MIRSD-PoD/P/CIR/2025/97 dated July 02, 2025

Attention !!!!

As per SEBI circular No. : SEBI/HO/MIRSD/MIRSD-PoD/P/CIR/2025/97 dated July 02, 2025, a special window is available for a period of six months from July 07, 2025 till January 06, 2026 for re-lodgement of transfer deeds, which were lodged prior to the deadline of April 01, 2019 and rejected / returned.
